Wow Celebration

Are you bored of the same old conventional parties with same old games ? Dying to make your party the highlight on everyone ‘s Instagram profile ? We as event planners have scrapped four coins together and brought some special tips for you ! There are always minute differences between a a normal party and a WOW party .. These are the meticulously planned little ecstatic details that matter that make any party worth remembering by your guests. After following these little tips the aunties and uncles at your party will not be able to stop their tongue from swagging. If you want your party to be example or a benchmark of your family ‘s intimate parties just remember all of your guests are important. So our PRO-TIPS on the anvil as follows :

1. COORDINATED EFFORTS:

Always make sure , all the preparations in an event are well coordinated. Pick up a colour or a theme and stick to it. It should well start from the invitations and end till the THANK YOU message. It can be a theme , a colour , a pattern or a style. Make sure all your creatives relating to an event follow the same and reflect the same.

2. MAKE ENTRY SPECIAL :

Entrance is the first impression the guests will have of an event. It has the ability to cause storm in their brains , make sure to make it a highlighting point. Use customized entry boards, flowers , balloons , foliage and don’t deviant from the theme.

3. WELCOMING THE GUEST IN STYLE :

Be it a small party or a big wedding reception , make sure to welcome your guests in style. Have the welcome as an aliasion to the selected theme. Make sure not to load them with things that they can’t carry. Example make a stamp for your event , cost effective as well as it will stand out. Have them wear bands or paint a logo in their hands . Give them quirky badges to go with theme.
Give them a small hand fan to carry and use as well or a mala but make sure to make it look pretty to go with their dresses. Have well dressed and groomed hostess welcome them at entrance if it’s a larger event or a family member if a smaller event.
To be A lil bit extra , have a little game related to the things you give at the entry. Like ( LUCKY DRAW , or GIFT partners or Seating Style etc.

4. PERSONALISED IS THE NEW CUSTOMISED :

Make the look of the party as customized and relatable to the hosts and guests as possible. Use their favourite hobby/show/movie as the theme. Use their favourite dialogues or actors as the tags in parties. Let the personality of the host of the party be reflected in every element guest notices at the party.

5. EVENT SPECIFIC PLAYLIST :

Make sure to have event specific playlist. This may sound a bit simple and obvious to all , but the difference that this step will make is humongous. Make the playlist with all the favourite songs of the host of the party to make the songs relatable to him/her. Or choose songs to go with the mood of the party or the specific theme of the party. If it’s a tropical or hawaiian theme party make sure to introduce your guests to the hawaiian cultural songs etc, if it’s a back to a School themed party make sure to put lots of school jingles and poems in the playlist etc. This songs can be played at the entry , between the games , while having food and at exit. It surely adds to the zing of the celebration and creates mood.

6. FILLERS :

Picking up of any event after its starting time is the most difficult thing. Make sure to use fillers as in games that involve the guests while the host make the entry and the party kick starts. Small games , self played things , riddle challenge , asking them to fill the message or wish counters. Make a talking booth , a red carpet or a message counter. Make them a part and ask them questions about the host of the party. Having A Karaoke counter can be a boon.

7. Designated Event Flow :

Make sure to have a pre-decided event flow , along with time and required songs and materials. Make sure to keep the games in coordination with the themes and choices of the guests. This will give you a clear idea of spending your time at an event, and you will not let any random guest take mic and mess with your event flow. Make sure the games are in full sync with the theme and mood of the event.
Prepare Excel sheet for the number of gifts required, for the guests as winners, number of blasts materials required .List down which game will suit which guest. So that you don’t end up calling shy guests to the over-enthusiastic games.

8. DIY COUNTER :

Make sure to include a DIY ACTIVITY or A FOOD counter to help guest stay involved even when they are free and roaming. Make sure to award the best DIY food preparer or activity completer.
