About us

Happily There to Help you in all your celebrations.

Sharing Hustles is our Favourite job!

We, at KARTKREATIONZ – THE EVENT HUB : are there at your rescue to make sure that even though your life would be full of ups and downs , your celebration won’t be. We are there to make sure your celebration is nothing less than what you dreamt of. Planning the smallest nitty-gritties of your celebration , making sure that even though you are hustling everyday in your life : on the day of celebration you are your boss while we serve you with happiness and all the fun.

Be it your fairy tale dream or Tech drama : be it as similar to chalk and cheese . We make sure we serve you with latest trends and and give you all the dreamy drama you wished for in every aspect of your celebration. KARTKREATIONZ is the dreamchild of POOJA JOGANI and NIKITA THANKI , founded on MAY 2 , 2017. Its been 2 years our Ladybosses have been doing what they love with some of the best celebrations tucked under their belt : Styling , conceptualising , managing , executing your celebratory events and finding all the comfort in chaos.
